## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-11

### Added

- Initial release of SwParticles as an open-source iOS library.
- CocoaPods (`SwParticles.podspec`) and Swift Package Manager (`Package.swift`) distribution.
- Particle engine, emitters, behaviours, zones, pooling, and SwiftUI-oriented renderers under `Sources/SwParticles/`.
